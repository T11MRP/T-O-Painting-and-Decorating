What we do

Decorating, done end to end

Every job starts with proper preparation and ends with a clean finish and a tidy space. Here's the work we take on.

Interior painting & decorating

Walls, ceilings, woodwork and trim across whole homes or single rooms — including bold feature walls and full-colour schemes.

Exterior painting

Render, masonry, fascias and exterior woodwork prepared and finished to stand up to the British weather.

Wallpaper & feature walls

Hung straight, matched and finished — from a single statement wall to a full room, papered or painted.

Wood & timber treatment

Decking, summerhouses, fencing and garden timber cleaned, treated and re-coated to bring the grain back to life.
